It's that time of year where we gather fresh ideas for Mozilla's next Internet Health Report.

The Internet Health Report is a check up on the internet. It includes research and stories from people all over the world on how the internet is working for good—or bad—in their lives.

We'd like to invite you to submit your ideas before September 14.

We're mainly looking for research, articles, and inspiring initiatives to make the internet healthier, but you can also just suggest an issue you don't hear enough about.

We accept ideas on our website (anonymous or public) or by Twitter message (use the hashtag #internethealth).

We hope you enjoyed the 2018 Internet Health Report.

We look forward to creating the next one with you.

Best,

Solana Larsen
Editor, Internet Health Report
