If you're reading this, you're one of the nearly 50% of people in the world on the internet. That also means 50% of the world's population still isn't online.

Surprised by that? Were you surprised that 50 million Facebook users had their data used by Cambridge Analytica for partisan political purposes without their knowledge?

Technology is advancing faster than we can often keep up. It's hard to get a handle on the opportunities and challenges. People feel vulnerable to fake news and open to security threats online everyday. All the while, shoes can order pizza, and artificial intelligence helps doctors diagnose disease outbreaks faster than ever. The health of the internet is not just a technical issue, it's a human one.

Just how healthy is our internet? Check out the 2018 Internet Health Report.

Through data, research and stories we show how the internet is evolving. What does 'security' mean in a world where our phones, watches, cars, and vacuums are all online? What is driving the onslaught of fake news? How big is 'too big' when it comes to tech giants?
